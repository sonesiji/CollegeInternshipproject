About Christ University, Bangalore

Christ (Deemed to be University), a premier educational institution, was established in 1969 as Christ College, Bangalore. Located in the heart of the city, Christ University is known for its academic excellence and is renowned as one of the leading institutions in India. The campus spans lush greenery, providing a peaceful environment for its students, while being well-connected to the major hubs of Bangalore.

Christ University offers undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs in a wide variety of disciplines such as Arts, Science, Commerce, Management, Law, Engineering, and Social Sciences. It has always been at the forefront of promoting academic excellence combined with social responsibility, holistic education, and global competency. The university consistently ranks among the top universities in the country for its dynamic curriculum, dedicated faculty, and state-of-the-art infrastructure.

The university was granted autonomy in 2004 by the University Grants Commission (UGC) and became a deemed-to-be university in 2008. Christ University is known for its emphasis on discipline, social responsibility, leadership development, and the overall growth of students. With over 25,000 students from across India and abroad, the campus truly reflects diversity and inclusivity. The institution's motto, 'Excellence and Service', underpins all its initiatives, as it strives to contribute to nation-building and societal progress.
